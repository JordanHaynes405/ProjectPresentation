Why did we choose the Course Calendar Creator?
  • It seemed within our collective capacity
  • We felt it could provide skills on automating reports
  • Nobody else was up for the challenge, it was ours
Why did we choose Impress.js?
  • It was the inspiration for Prezi
  • Unlike Prezi it was 3-dimentional
  • We wanted to learn to search for
    and add on pre-made scripts
